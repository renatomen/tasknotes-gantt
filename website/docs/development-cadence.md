# Development Cadence

*An honest note about pace.* 💜

I built TaskNotes Gantt to scratch my own itch, and I'm glad to share it with
anyone it helps. But I have very little time for it — and I won't let a joyful
project turn into a burdensome one.

I genuinely welcome community feedback, requests, and bug reports, and I care
about them. I just can't promise to answer quickly, so please keep that in mind
before you come to rely on this plugin.

Here's the reassuring part: **the plugin is solid.** Every shipped feature is
backed by thorough unit and integration tests and my own daily use. And moving
slowly is *precisely* how I keep it that way. That, more than anything, is the
real reason for the **Beta** label — the code is steady; it's the *support* that's
beta, and I'd rather be honest about that than leave you refreshing an issue
thread.

Over time I hope this becomes more community-driven and co-evolves with
**[TaskNotes](https://tasknotes.dev)** — see [Vision & Philosophy](vision-and-philosophy.md)
for where I'd love it to go. For now: thank you for your patience, and I'm really
glad it's useful to you. 🙏
